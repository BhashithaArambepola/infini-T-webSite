About Us

Infini-T Tech Solutions is a Solutions providing company based in Colombo, Sri Lanka. Our main focus to provide IT solutions and services to Clients Worldwide. With our highly talented experience consultants and developers we ensure to provide the best solution for our client’s requirements and help organizations for their Digital Transformation. We provide Microsoft Dynamics ERP Implementations & Development and Consultancy & Support for Microsoft products. With our development we are provide bespoke Software and Mobile Application solutions to our clients.
